# Personas

## Primary Personas

### The Curious Fan

**Name:** Sam, 28
**Location:** Manchester
**Behavior:** Goes to 2-3 gigs per month, mostly discovers through word of mouth or Instagram. Wishes they knew about more local music.

**Goals:**
- Find interesting gigs happening nearby
- Discover new artists before they blow up
- Feel connected to the local music scene

**Frustrations:**
- Finds out about good gigs after they've happened
- Facebook events are cluttered with irrelevant stuff
- Hard to know which small venues have good programming

**bndy job:** Discovery feed, location-based recommendations, "tonight" quick view

---

### The Community Builder

**Name:** Roz, 35
**Location:** Bristol
**Role:** Runs a 150-cap venue and promotes DIY shows

**Goals:**
- Fill rooms with the right audience
- Build reputation for their venue/brand
- Connect with artists to book

**Frustrations:**
- Reaching beyond existing followers is hard
- No good analytics on who attends and why
- Coordinating with artists on promotion is chaotic

**bndy job:** Venue/promoter profile, event submission, audience analytics, artist discovery

---

### The Emerging Artist

**Name:** Jaz, 24
**Location:** Leeds
**Role:** Plays in an indie band, books own shows

**Goals:**
- Get booked at more venues
- Build a fanbase outside their city
- Connect with other artists for bills

**Frustrations:**
- Cold-emailing venues gets ignored
- Hard to prove draw without historic data
- No central place to showcase gig history

**bndy job:** Artist profile, gig history, connection to venues/promoters

---

## Secondary Personas

### The Scene Tourist

Visiting a city, wants to find authentic local music tonight. Needs hyper-local, immediate recommendations.

### The Music Journalist

Covers local scenes, needs to track emerging artists and venues. Wants data and trends.

### The Label Scout

Looking for unsigned talent. Wants to see who's playing where, building buzz.
