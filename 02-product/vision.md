# Product Vision

## The One-Liner

**bndy helps you discover live music you didn't know existed.**

## The Problem We Solve

You want to see live music tonight. You open Facebook - nothing. Instagram - a flyer from 3 days ago. Google - Ticketmaster results for arena shows 50 miles away.

Meanwhile, there's a brilliant jazz trio playing at a wine bar 10 minutes away. A punk band in someone's basement. An open mic at the pub. You'll never know.

Grassroots music is invisible because it's fragmented across dozens of sources, none of which are designed for discovery.

## Our Solution

bndy aggregates, understands, and surfaces grassroots music events:

1. **Ingest** events from venue websites, social media, ticketing platforms, community submissions
2. **Understand** the entities involved - which venue, which artist, what genre, what vibe
3. **Surface** relevant events to users based on location, taste, and context
4. **Connect** fans with community builders who curate local scenes

## Who We Serve

| Persona | Need | bndy Value |
|---------|------|-----------|
| **Music fans** | Find gigs worth attending | Discovery feed, recommendations |
| **Community builders** | Promote their events/venues | Reach new audiences, analytics |
| **Artists** | Get discovered, build fanbase | Visibility, connection to venues |

## What We're Not

- **Not a ticketing platform** - We link to tickets, we don't sell them
- **Not a streaming service** - We care about live music, not recordings
- **Not a social network** - We're a discovery layer, not a feed

## Success Looks Like

- A user opens bndy and finds 3 gigs they want to attend this week
- A venue owner sees 20% of their audience discovered them via bndy
- An artist gets booked at a new venue because a promoter found them on bndy
